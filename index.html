Why Amsterdam?

Amsterdam, fits everybody

Visit the world-famous, 17th century capital of The Netherlands. Enjoy the historic buildings, beautiful museums and pleasant ambiance. Go for a boat tour on the canals, stroll through Vondelpark, and go shopping in the inner city. Amsterdam is a unique city! Many famous painters lived here, and you can find many famous works of art in the museums. Amsterdam is a party capital for young people as it has a lot of parties, restaurants and fun night life. During the day you can enjoy attractions in the city like the wonder experience, eat well known cookie or the famous apple pie and swing up high in the A'dam Tower.
